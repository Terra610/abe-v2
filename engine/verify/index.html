A.B.E. — Verify Audit Hash

Confirm that your saved audit block has not been altered since the receipt was created.

Step 1 — Paste your export block

Paste the **exact export block JSON** you previously saved. If even one character is different, the hash will not match.

This should match what you copied from /engine/export/ when you generated your hash.

Step 2 — Paste your recorded SHA-256 hash

Paste the SHA-256 hash from your receipt or notes. The engine will recompute the hash of the export block and compare.

If the result is VALID, your export block is bit-for-bit identical to what existed when the hash was created.
